# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## ✅ Cyber Safety Checklist for College Students

- [ ] Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, CVV, or passwords with anyone.
- [ ] Do not enter your UPI PIN to **receive money**; PIN is only needed to send money.
- [ ] Always verify UPI collect requests before approving them.
- [ ] Avoid clicking unknown links received through SMS, WhatsApp, email, or social media.
- [ ] Use strong and unique passwords for email, banking, and social accounts.
- [ ] Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts.
- [ ] Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or official websites.
- [ ] Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online banking or UPI transactions.
- [ ] Check website URLs carefully before logging in or making payments.
- [ ] Never share personal documents like Aadhaar, PAN, or college ID online unnecessarily.

## 💳 Financial / UPI Safety Tips

- [ ] Always check the name and UPI ID of the receiver before sending money.
- [ ] Never scan random QR codes sent by unknown people for “receiving payment”.
- [ ] Immediately block your bank account or UPI service if fraud is suspected.

## 🚨 Reporting Channel

If you face any cyber fraud, report immediately:

- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** cybercrime.gov.in
- **Cyber Crime Helpline:** 1930
